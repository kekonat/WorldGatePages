Why This Continuation Exists

After catching up to Chapter 88, I was devastated to see that the original fan translation had stopped updating as of June 8, 2025. I love Tang San’s works too much to leave one of his stories unfinished.

This project was started so readers could continue the adventure beyond that point. Care has been taken to keep terminology as consistent as possible with shunshunrika’s translation, with a small number of adjustments made to better align with the official translation of Douluo Dalu 5: Rebirth of Tang San.

May this continuation carry the same excitement, emotion, and sense of discovery as the journey unfolds.
